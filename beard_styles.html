Beard Fade

It was only a matter of time before fades became a part of facial hair. This unique look features a clean line on the cheek that fades down to the jawline. Facial hair is left in a goatee and a beautifully arced chin strap. This bold look would work for patch beard growth as well as neck beards.

Short Beard + Full Mustache

Every guy’s facial grows in a bit differently. This gentleman makes the most of thicker growth in the mustache and thinner hair on the cheeks. A light fade at the sideburn creates a seamless connection to facial hair.

Trimmed Beard Style

Now that’s how to trim a beard. This thick beard is groomed to perfection with a clean line right below the jaw, graceful arc from the mouth to temple and short mustache. And then there is the skin fade at the temple and shaved design behind the ear.

Trimmed Beard Styled

Bald heads plus beards are always in style. This combination draws extra focus to facial hair so it’s an opportunity to get a unique groomed style. This geometric look combines a triangular beard shape with a square cut out. The slim mustache and faded sides complete the look. This linear look is a cool beard style for round faces.

Small Beard + Faded Sideburns

This gentleman haircut worn with a small beard gets a modern update from a quick fade through the sideburns.

Full Beard + Long Mustache

This enviable beard groomed into a rounded shape also has an impressive mustache. Hair is worn long and groomed up and out but not twisted into handlebars.

Full Beard + Handlebar Mustache

This lumberjack beard is ready for the city thanks to professional grooming, a dapper hairstyle and a slight waxed handlebar at the ends of a full mustache.
